FAQs

By when can the financier interest and hypothecation be terminated, post making full loan payment?

The termination of financier interest is to be made within 03 months of issuance of NOC and Form 35 otherwise the fresh NoC and Form 35 is to be re- issued after certain payment to the financier.

When and why do I have to pay One Time Tax and / or Green Tax again?

At the time of Sale of vehicle, OTT needs to be paid again when the vehicle is transferred in the new owner name. The Green Tax is applicable upto 15 years of vehicle registration, and needs to be paid again post the stipulated time. This is mandatory for all types of vehicles.
