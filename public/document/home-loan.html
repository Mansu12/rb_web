Property Documents if purchasing from Builder

  • Copy of latest sale agreement or Draft Agreement Copy
  • BMC or other Authority approved plan copy {If OC is not Available}; CC Copy
  • OC Copy {in some cases you may have OC}
  • Payment Schedule and receipts of payments from Builder
  • Allotment letter from Builder
  • Builder NOC in Bank Format on Letter Head. {We will send to Customer}
  • {CIDCO or other authority Area NOC is requires in their Format with Bank name}
  • Other as per Legal report of Bank

Property Documents if Resale Property

  • Copy of all Previous Chain Agreement
  • Copy of latest sale Agreement or Draft Agreement Copy
  • Share Certificate Front & Back Side Copy {If Issued}
  • In some banks Transfer form will be needed
  • Copy of Stamp duty and Registration Recites of all previous Chain Agreement
  • OC Copy; Copy of BMC Approved Plan & CC Copy{If OC is not Available}
  • Society NOC in Bank Format on Letter Head (post sanction)
  • {CIDCO or other authority Area NOC is requires in their Format with Bank name}
