Required Documents (Credit Card)

All copies should be OSV and KYC Customer attested

Salaried

Customer KYC - Valid Passport
Voter ID Card
Aadhaar Card
Valid Driving Licence
Proof of income - Last 3 months' Salary Slips
Last 6 months' Bank Statements, showing salary credits
Statement of current credit cards availed by the customer

Self Employed

Customer and Company KYCCustomer KYC - Valid Passport
(Partners/Directors KYC wherever applicable Voter ID Card
Aadhaar Card
Valid Driving Licence
Proof of income - Last 6 months' Current A/c Statements of the business entity and Savings Account Statements of the individual

Conditions for Self Employed**

Fresh cards issued only to the Directors of the company.

For other self-employed, only the existing credit card holders shall be provided with the additional cards (no fresh cards issued).
